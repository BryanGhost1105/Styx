Frequently Asked Questions

How exactly does the data encryption work?

We use AES-256 bank-grade encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit. Styx operates using ephemeral context windows—meaning your data is processed, orchestrated, and then permanently cleared from our memory registers.

Does it actually support my entire tech stack?

Styx integrates with 1,200+ applications via OAuth, API, and webhook. If your tool has a digital heartbeat, Styx can orchestrate it. From legacy ERPs to modern AI wrappers.

How 'autonomous' is the orchestration?

Styx operates on a human-defined threshold. You can set it to "Full Autonomy" for repetitive low-risk tasks, or "Review Required" for high-impact decisions like client communications or budget allocation.

Is there a setup fee for enterprise teams?

No setup fees. We provide a dedicated solutions engineer for all Enterprise trials to help map your first 3 complex logic flows at no additional cost.
